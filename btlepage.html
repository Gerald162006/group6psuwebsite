BTLED (Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education): The Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education program is geared towards producing educators with expertise in livelihood and technical skills. It combines teaching methods with practical training in areas such as home economics, ICT, agriculture, and entrepreneurship. These programs serve as a foundation for professional growth, equipping students with specialized knowledge and practical expertise to succeed in their chosen fields.
