Welcome to the BTLE Program

A Bachelor of Technical and Livelihood Education (BTLE) is a 4-year undergraduate program focused on preparing students to teach technical and vocational subjects at the secondary or post-secondary level.

Focus: Prepares teachers to instruct in fields such as industrial arts, home economics, agriculture, and other livelihood skills. Teaching Levels: Secondary schools, vocational institutions, and similar educational settings.

Core Subjects: Foundations of education, educational psychology, curriculum design, teaching methods, assessment. Technical Subjects: Vocational education (e.g., agriculture, industrial arts), practical skills development, livelihood education. Practical Training: Student teaching, fieldwork, and internship experiences in technical settings.

Career Opportunities: Technical Education Teacher Vocational Instructor Curriculum Developer Educational Consultant Technical Skills Trainer

The program duration typically lasts 3-4 years, depending on the country and institution..
