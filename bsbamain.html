Welcome to the BSBA Program

A Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) is a 3-4 year undergraduate degree focusing on IT principles and skills. Curriculum Highlights: Programming (Python, Java, C++), web/app development, algorithms, networking, cybersecurity, AI, IT project management. Career Paths: Software development, data analysis, cybersecurity, IT support, cloud computing. Specializations: AI, cloud computing, game development, and more.

A Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) is a 4-year undergraduate program focused on business fundamentals and management. Core Subjects:

Career Opportunities:Management, accounting, business law, economics, organizational behavior. Specializations: Marketing, finance, HR, entrepreneurship, international business, operations. Career Paths: Business analyst, marketing manager, HR manager, financial analyst, entrepreneur, operations manager.

Advanced Opportunities: Many graduates pursue an MBA for career advancement.
