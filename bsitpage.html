Course Title: Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

College Dean: Dr. Jb O. Doria

Show Course Details

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) is an undergraduate degree program designed to prepare students for a professional career in the dynamic field of technology and computing. The program focuses on the application, development, and management of information technology solutions to address real-world challenges in businesses, organizations, and various industries. With courses ranging from programming, web development, data management, network security, to emerging technologies like artificial intelligence and cloud computing, the BSIT program equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Emphasizing problem-solving, critical thinking, and innovation, it prepares graduates for roles such as IT specialists, software developers, system administrators, cybersecurity experts, and more. Tailored for the demands of the modern digital landscape, BSIT offers students the opportunity to specialize in areas of interest, engage in hands-on projects, and build a strong foundation for advanced studies or certifications.
