Welcome to the BSE Program

A Bachelor of Science in Education (BSE) is a 4-year undergraduate program designed to prepare students for teaching careers by developing skills in pedagogy, child development, and education theories.

Core Courses: Foundations of education, educational psychology, classroom management, curriculum development, assessment, and technology in education. Specializations: Elementary education, secondary education (specific subjects), special education, and physical education. Practical Training: Includes student teaching and internships in real-world classrooms.

Career Oppotunities: Elementary or secondary school teacher Special education teacher Curriculum developer Educational consultant School administrator (with additional qualifications)

The program duration typically lasts 3-4 years, depending on the country and institution..
