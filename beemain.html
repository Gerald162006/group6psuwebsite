Welcome to the BEED Program

A Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd) is a 4-year undergraduate program focused on preparing students to teach young children in elementary schools (grades 1-6).

Focus: Prepares students to teach multiple subjects using a holistic, student-centered approach. Specializations: General elementary education, special education (SPED), early childhood education. Curriculum

Core Courses: Child development, teaching principles, curriculum development, assessment, classroom management, technology in education. Subject-Specific Pedagogy: Mathematics, science, English, Filipino, social studies, music, arts, physical education (MAPEH). Practical Training: Field study, student teaching/internship.

Career Opportunities: Elementary School Teacher Early Childhood Educator Special Education Teacher Curriculum Specialist Educational Materials Developer
